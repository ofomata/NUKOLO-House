Coffee Produced in Mexico

Our coffee is a more premium SHB (Strictly Hard Bean) specifies that the coffee was grown at an altitude above 1350 meters. Higher altitude bean cultivation results in denser beans with superior specialty cup quality.

Our Specialities

Some of our very special varities as listed below

Premium/Specialty Roast: Café Milano / Coffee Milano

Origin: Chiapas, Mx

This Milano coffee is ideal for Americano percolator preparations because of its intense profile to burnt caramel, dark chocolate and a touch of acidity.

Premium/Specialty Roast: Café Dulce Crema | Coffee Sweet Cream

Origin: Oaxaca, Mx

100% Mexican Arabic grains from the upper area of Oaxaca, with a low acidity profile without being aggressive to the palate, great sweetness, with nuances of caramel, vanilla and chocolate with a creamy texture and full body.
This cafe is profiled for espressos and milk drinks like cappuccinos and lattes.
